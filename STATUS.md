# Space Launch System / Orion — Program Status

> As of December 5, 2014

**NASA Administrator:** Charles F. Bolden Jr. (12th Administrator)
**Goal:** Asteroid Redirect Mission (capture near-Earth asteroid); Mars in the 2030s
**Next Milestone:** SLS core stage production; EM-1 first flight (November 2018 target)

## Vehicles
- **Space Launch System (SLS): Passed KDP-C — full development authorized, EM-1 targeted November 2018**
- **Orion MPCV: EFT-1 complete — 4.5-hour uncrewed test flight successful**

## Overview
Orion has completed its first flight test (EFT-1) on a Delta IV Heavy, reaching an altitude of 3,600 miles and testing the heat shield at 80% of lunar return speed. The capsule was recovered in the Pacific. SLS continues toward EM-1.
