# Space Launch System / Orion — Program Status

> As of April 27, 2017

**NASA Administrator:** Robert M. Lightfoot Jr. (Acting Administrator)
**Goal:** Mars in the 2030s (via cislunar proving ground)
**Next Milestone:** EM-1 crew study results; SLS EM-1 first flight

## Vehicles
- **Space Launch System (SLS): EM-1 likely to slip past November 2018 to 2019 (GAO)**
- **Orion MPCV: EFT-1 complete — 4.5-hour uncrewed test flight successful**

## Overview
A GAO report has found that SLS EM-1 will likely slip past November 2018 into 2019 due to core stage production challenges. ESM delivery and the Boeing core stage are the primary schedule drivers.
