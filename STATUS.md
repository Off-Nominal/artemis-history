# Artemis — Program Status

> As of November 16, 2022

**NASA Administrator:** Bill Nelson (14th Administrator)
**Goal:** Land the first woman and first person of color on the Moon (2024 target abandoned)
**Next Milestone:** Orion lunar flyby, distant retrograde orbit, and return to Earth

## Vehicles
- **Space Launch System (SLS): FIRST FLIGHT — launched November 16, 2022**
- **Orion MPCV: In flight — en route to the Moon**
- **Human Landing System: SpaceX Starship HLS — $2.89B + $1.15B Option B for Artemis III & IV**
- **Lunar Gateway: Removed from Artemis III critical path — not required for first landing**

## International Partners
Artemis Accords: 8 founding nations (U.S., Australia, Canada, Italy, Japan, Luxembourg, UAE, UK)

## Overview
ARTEMIS I HAS LAUNCHED. SLS completed its maiden flight, sending Orion toward the Moon at 1:47 AM EST from LC-39B. All systems nominal. Orion will enter a distant retrograde orbit around the Moon before returning for a Pacific splashdown.
