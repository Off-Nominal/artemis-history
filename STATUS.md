# Artemis — Program Status

> As of October 20, 2025

**NASA Administrator:** Sean Duffy (Acting Administrator (also Secretary of Transportation))
**Goal:** Return Americans to the Moon
**Next Milestone:** Evaluate HLS acceleration proposals; Artemis II rollout

## Vehicles
- **Space Launch System (SLS): Artemis II stacking begun at VAB**
- **Orion MPCV: Heat shield root cause identified — AVCOAT permeability; fixes being implemented**
- **Human Landing System: COMPETITION REOPENED — Duffy ends SpaceX sole-source for Artemis III**
- **Lunar Gateway: HALO arrived for integration with PPE at Northrop Grumman**

## International Partners
Artemis Accords: 50 signatory nations

## Overview
Acting Administrator Duffy has opened the Artemis III lander to competition, citing Starship delays and the race against China's 2030 lunar landing goal. SpaceX and Blue Origin must submit acceleration proposals by October 29. The decision drew criticism from Elon Musk but support from former administrators.
