# Space Launch System / Orion — Program Status

> As of March 21, 2017

**NASA Administrator:** Robert M. Lightfoot Jr. (Acting Administrator)
**Goal:** Mars in the 2030s (via cislunar proving ground)
**Next Milestone:** EM-1 crew study results; SLS EM-1 first flight

## Vehicles
- **Space Launch System (SLS): Passed KDP-C — full development authorized, EM-1 targeted November 2018**
- **Orion MPCV: EFT-1 complete — 4.5-hour uncrewed test flight successful**

## Overview
The NASA Transition Authorization Act of 2017 affirms NASA's long-range goal of crewed Mars missions in the 2030s while maintaining SLS and Orion. The act does not mention the Asteroid Redirect Mission, signaling waning support for ARM.
