# Artemis — Program Status

> As of April 3, 2023

**NASA Administrator:** Bill Nelson (14th Administrator)
**Goal:** Land the first woman and first person of color on the Moon (2024 target abandoned)
**Next Milestone:** Heat shield resolution; Artemis II vehicle integration; Starship HLS development

## Vehicles
- **Space Launch System (SLS): Flight-proven**
- **Orion MPCV: Artemis II crew named — Wiseman, Glover, Koch, Hansen (CSA)**
- **Human Landing System: SpaceX Starship HLS — $2.89B + $1.15B Option B for Artemis III & IV**
- **Lunar Gateway: Removed from Artemis III critical path — not required for first landing**

## International Partners
Artemis Accords: 8 founding nations (U.S., Australia, Canada, Italy, Japan, Luxembourg, UAE, UK)

## Overview
NASA has named the Artemis II crew: Commander Reid Wiseman, Pilot Victor Glover, Mission Specialists Christina Koch and Jeremy Hansen (CSA). This will be the first crewed SLS/Orion flight — a lunar flyby. The heat shield investigation continues.
