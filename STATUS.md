# Space Launch System / Orion — Program Status

> As of October 8, 2015

**NASA Administrator:** Charles F. Bolden Jr. (12th Administrator)
**Goal:** Moon vicinity (proving ground) → cislunar space → Mars (mid-2030s)
**Next Milestone:** SLS core stage production; EM-1 first flight (November 2018 target)

## Vehicles
- **Space Launch System (SLS): Passed KDP-C — full development authorized, EM-1 targeted November 2018**
- **Orion MPCV: EFT-1 complete — 4.5-hour uncrewed test flight successful**

## Overview
NASA has published its 'Journey to Mars' strategy: Earth Reliant (ISS through 2024), Proving Ground (cislunar space in the 2020s), and Earth Independent (Mars in the 2030s). The Asteroid Redirect Mission is a key Proving Ground milestone. SLS and Orion continue toward EM-1.
