# Space Launch System / Orion — Program Status

> As of February 15, 2017

**NASA Administrator:** Robert M. Lightfoot Jr. (Acting Administrator)
**Goal:** Moon vicinity (proving ground) → cislunar space → Mars (mid-2030s)
**Next Milestone:** EM-1 crew feasibility study (in progress); SLS core stage production

## Vehicles
- **Space Launch System (SLS): Passed KDP-C — full development authorized, EM-1 targeted November 2018**
- **Orion MPCV: EFT-1 complete — 4.5-hour uncrewed test flight successful**

## Overview
Acting Administrator Lightfoot has ordered a feasibility study into adding crew to EM-1, potentially turning the uncrewed test flight into a crewed lunar flyby. The idea originated from the Trump transition team. Adding crew would require a life support system, abort capability, and upgraded software — none of which are in the current EM-1 plan.
