# SpaceX HLS Option B - Artemis IV

## Contract Award: November 15, 2022

**Value**: $1.15 billion (contract modification)
**Provider**: SpaceX

NASA awards SpaceX the Option B contract modification for a second
crewed lunar landing mission, building on the original $2.89B Option A
contract from April 2021.

## Scope

Develop a Starship lunar lander meeting NASA's "sustaining" requirements
for missions beyond Artemis III:

- Capability to dock with Lunar Gateway
- Accommodate four crew members (vs. two for Artemis III)
- Deliver more mass to the lunar surface
- Provide a second crewed demonstration landing (Artemis IV)

## Total SpaceX HLS Contract Value
- Option A (April 2021): $2.89 billion (Artemis III)
- Option B (November 2022): $1.15 billion (Artemis IV)
- **Total: ~$4.04 billion**

## Program Structure (as of Nov 2022)
1. **SpaceX Starship HLS** - Artemis III (demo) and Artemis IV
2. **Blue Origin Blue Moon MK2** - Artemis V and beyond (awarded May 2023)
