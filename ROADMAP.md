# Space Launch System / Orion — Roadmap

> Forward-looking plan as of August 7, 2014

## Upcoming Milestones

- **December 5, 2014**: Orion EFT-1 test flight on Delta IV Heavy
- **November 2018**: SLS/Orion EM-1 — first flight (uncrewed lunar flyby)
- **Early 2020s**: ARM robotic mission
- **~2022–2023**: EM-2 — first crewed SLS/Orion flight
- **Mid-2020s**: Crewed visit to redirected asteroid
- **2030s**: Crewed Mars missions

## Long-Term Vision

EFT-1 is imminent. SLS has entered full development with a baselined launch date.
