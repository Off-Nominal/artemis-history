# Artemis — Roadmap

> Forward-looking plan as of December 11, 2022

## Upcoming Milestones

- **Late 2024**: Artemis II — first crewed SLS/Orion lunar flyby (4 crew)
- **2025–2026**: SpaceX uncrewed Starship HLS lunar landing demonstration
- **2025–2026**: Artemis III — first crewed lunar landing
- **2025+**: Gateway PPE + HALO launch
- **2027+**: Artemis IV — Gateway utilization mission
- **2030s**: Mars

## Long-Term Vision

SLS and Orion are flight-proven. The next step is crewed flight, then the Moon.
