# Artemis — Roadmap

> Forward-looking plan as of May 23, 2019

## Upcoming Milestones

- **2020**: Artemis I — SLS/Orion uncrewed lunar flyby
- **~2022**: Artemis II — first crewed SLS/Orion lunar flyby
- **2024**: Artemis III — first crewed lunar landing (first woman and next man)
- **2023–2024**: Lunar Gateway initial elements (PPE + mini-hab)
- **2024+**: Sustained lunar surface operations
- **2030s**: Human missions to Mars

## Artemis Program Architecture

1. **SLS** launches Orion crew to lunar orbit
2. **Gateway** (small lunar orbit station) serves as staging point
3. **Human Landing System** (commercial, TBD) carries crew to the surface
4. Crew returns to Orion via Gateway for Earth return

## Long-Term Vision

Artemis: land the first woman and next man on the Moon by 2024, establish sustainable exploration by 2028, and prepare for human Mars missions in the 2030s.
