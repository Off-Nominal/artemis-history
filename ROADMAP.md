# Artemis — Roadmap

> Forward-looking plan as of November 9, 2021

## Upcoming Milestones

- **2022**: Artemis I — uncrewed lunar flyby (integration underway)
- **2024**: SpaceX uncrewed Starship HLS demo landing
- **~2024**: Artemis II — crewed lunar flyby
- **2025–2026**: Artemis III — first crewed lunar landing
- **2025+**: Gateway PPE + HALO launch
- **2025+**: Second HLS provider selection
- **2030s**: Mars

## Long-Term Vision

The landing will happen, but likely 2025–2026 at the earliest.
