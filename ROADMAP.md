# Artemis — Roadmap

> Forward-looking plan as of June 20, 2022

## Upcoming Milestones

- **August–September 2022**: Artemis I LAUNCH
- **2024**: Artemis II crewed lunar flyby
- **2025–2026**: Artemis III — first crewed lunar landing
- **2025+**: Gateway PPE + HALO launch
- **2030s**: Mars

## Long-Term Vision

Artemis I launch is imminent. Success will validate SLS and Orion for crewed flights.
