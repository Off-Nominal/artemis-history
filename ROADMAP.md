# Space Launch System / Orion — Roadmap

> Forward-looking plan as of December 11, 2017

## Upcoming Milestones

- **June 2020**: SLS/Orion EM-1 — first flight (uncrewed lunar flyby)
- **~2023**: EM-2 — first crewed mission (lunar flyby)
- **Late 2020s**: Return humans to the lunar surface
- **2030s**: Human missions to Mars

## New Direction

SPD-1 has formally redirected NASA to the Moon. The program will develop a lunar orbital platform (Gateway) and a crewed landing system.

## Long-Term Vision

Sustainable lunar exploration as a proving ground for Mars. The Moon is now the stepping stone, not an asteroid.
