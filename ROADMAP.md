# Space Launch System / Orion — Roadmap

> Forward-looking plan as of April 10, 2013

## Upcoming Milestones

- **December 2014**: Orion EFT-1 test flight on Delta IV Heavy
- **Late 2017**: SLS/Orion EM-1 (uncrewed)
- **Early 2020s**: ARM robotic spacecraft captures asteroid / boulder
- **~2021**: First crewed SLS/Orion mission (EM-2)
- **Mid-2020s**: Astronauts visit redirected asteroid in lunar orbit
- **2030s**: Crewed Mars missions

## Long-Term Vision

ARM demonstrates key technologies for Mars: solar electric propulsion, crew operations in deep space, and asteroid sample collection.
