# Artemis — Roadmap

> Forward-looking plan as of January 20, 2020

## Upcoming Milestones

- **Late 2020**: SLS core stage Green Run test at Stennis
- **2021**: Artemis I — SLS/Orion uncrewed lunar flyby
- **2022**: HLS provider down-select
- **~2023**: Artemis II — crewed lunar flyby
- **2024**: Artemis III — FIRST CREWED LUNAR LANDING
- **2023–2024**: Gateway PPE + HALO launch
- **2030s**: Mars

## Long-Term Vision

Commercial landers will provide sustainable, reusable access to the lunar surface.
