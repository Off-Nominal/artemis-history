# Artemis — Roadmap

> Forward-looking plan as of May 19, 2023

## Upcoming Milestones

- **Late 2024**: Artemis II — crewed lunar flyby (pending heat shield resolution)
- **2025–2026**: SpaceX uncrewed Starship HLS demo
- **2025–2026**: Artemis III — first crewed landing (SpaceX Starship HLS)
- **2027+**: Artemis IV — SpaceX Option B landing
- **2028+**: Artemis V — Blue Origin Blue Moon MK2 landing
- **2025+**: Gateway PPE + HALO launch
- **2030s**: Mars

## Dual HLS Providers

1. **SpaceX Starship HLS** — Artemis III and IV
2. **Blue Origin Blue Moon MK2** — Artemis V and beyond

## Long-Term Vision

Two competing lander providers ensure sustainable, competitive lunar access.
