# Artemis — Roadmap

> Forward-looking plan as of November 4, 2021

## Upcoming Milestones

- **Late 2021**: Artemis I — uncrewed lunar flyby
- **2024**: SpaceX uncrewed Starship HLS lunar landing demonstration
- **~2024**: Artemis II — crewed lunar flyby
- **2025+**: Artemis III — first crewed lunar landing using Starship HLS
- **2025+**: Gateway PPE + HALO launch
- **2030s**: Mars

## Long-Term Vision

SpaceX selection brings massive cargo capacity but introduces orbital refueling as a critical new technology requirement.
