# Space Launch System / Orion — Roadmap

> Forward-looking plan as of March 26, 2019

## Upcoming Milestones

- **2020**: SLS/Orion first flight (Artemis I, uncrewed)
- **~2022**: Artemis II — first crewed SLS/Orion mission
- **2024**: Artemis III — LAND AMERICANS ON THE MOON
- **2024+**: Lunar Gateway operational
- **2028+**: Sustained lunar presence
- **2030s**: Human missions to Mars

## Long-Term Vision

Establish a sustained human presence on and around the Moon by the end of the decade, then use lunar experience to prepare for Mars.
