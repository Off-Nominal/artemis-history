# Artemis — Roadmap

> Forward-looking plan as of April 20, 2023

## Upcoming Milestones

- **Late 2024**: Artemis II — Wiseman, Glover, Koch, Hansen fly a 10-day crewed lunar flyby
- **2024–2025**: Heat shield fix implementation and verification
- **2025–2026**: SpaceX uncrewed Starship HLS demo; Artemis III crewed landing
- **2025**: Blue Origin second HLS provider selection (expected)
- **2025+**: Gateway PPE + HALO launch
- **2030s**: Mars

## Long-Term Vision

Crew is named. The next human deep-space mission is taking shape.
