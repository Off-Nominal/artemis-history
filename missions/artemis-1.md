# Artemis I

## Mission Overview
- **Type**: Uncrewed test flight
- **Vehicle**: SLS Block 1 + Orion + ESM-1
- **Launch Site**: LC-39B, Kennedy Space Center
- **Objective**: Test SLS and Orion systems in an integrated deep space flight

## Pre-Launch: Wet Dress Rehearsal

### Attempts
| Date | Result |
|------|--------|
| April 3, 2022 | Scrubbed - gaseous nitrogen supply issue |
| April 4, 2022 | Scrubbed - stuck valve on mobile launcher |
| April 14, 2022 | Scrubbed - liquid hydrogen leak |
| June 20, 2022 | Partial success - completed modified countdown |

The June 20 WDR achieved a simulated T-29 second countdown after resolving
previous hydrogen leak issues. NASA deemed the data sufficient to proceed
toward launch.

## Launch Window
Targeting August-September 2022.
