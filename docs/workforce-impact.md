# Artemis Program Workforce Impact (2025)

## DOGE Review

In February 2025, the Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE) begins
reviewing federal agencies including NASA as part of the new administration's
cost-reduction initiative.

## Impact on NASA Workforce

- ~870 NASA employees apply to leave in the first round of the Deferred
  Resignation Program
- ~3,000 apply in the second round
- Concerns raised about impacts on Artemis program workforce capacity
- Critical expertise in areas like SLS manufacturing, mission operations,
  and systems engineering potentially affected

## Artemis II Progress

Despite workforce concerns, Artemis II SRB stacking continues at KSC.
Booster stacking completed February 19, 2025.
