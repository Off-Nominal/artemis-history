# NASA Administrators During Artemis Era

## Jim Bridenstine (April 2018 - January 2021)
- Named the Artemis program (May 2019)
- Oversaw HLS solicitation and initial contract awards
- Pushed 2024 landing target per VP Pence directive
- Managed early SLS Green Run campaign

## Bill Nelson (May 3, 2021 - January 2025)
- Confirmed by Senate unanimous consent (April 29, 2021)
- Sworn in by VP Kamala Harris (May 3, 2021)
- Former U.S. Senator from Florida (2001-2019)
- Flew on Space Shuttle Columbia (STS-61-C, January 1986)
- Oversaw Artemis I launch and recovery
- Managed heat shield investigation
- Named Artemis II crew
- Acknowledged 2024 landing target not feasible (November 2021)
- Announced schedule delays following heat shield findings
