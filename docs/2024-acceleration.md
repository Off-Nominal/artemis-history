# 2024 Lunar Landing Acceleration

## Announced: March 26, 2019

Vice President Mike Pence, as chairman of the National Space Council,
announces that NASA's crewed lunar landing goal is accelerated from
2028 to 2024.

## Key Directives

- Land "the first woman and the next man" on the Moon by 2024
- Target the lunar south pole for the landing site
- Use all available means, commercial and government, to achieve the goal
- "By any means necessary" urgency language

## Implications

- Requires dramatic acceleration of Human Landing System development
- SLS/Orion timeline becomes even more critical
- Gateway scope may be reduced or deferred for initial missions
- Opens door to significant commercial sector involvement in landing system
