# Human Landing System (HLS) Solicitation

## NextSTEP-2 Appendix H (June 2019)

NASA issues a Broad Agency Announcement inviting industry proposals for
Human Landing System designs under the Next Space Technologies for
Exploration Partnerships (NextSTEP-2) program.

## Requirements

- Crew of 2 to the lunar surface
- Minimum 6.5-day surface stay capability
- Compatible with Orion and/or Gateway for crew transfer
- Capable of reaching the lunar south pole

## Industry Response

Multiple teams submit proposals, including:

- **SpaceX**: Starship-derived lunar lander
- **Blue Origin "National Team"**: Blue Moon lander (with Lockheed Martin,
  Northrop Grumman, Draper)
- **Dynetics**: Dynetics Human Landing System (DHLS)

## Evaluation Phase

NASA awards initial study contracts to all three teams in April 2020
for further development and risk reduction.
