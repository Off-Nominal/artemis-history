# Constellation Program Technical Overview

## Ares I
- Purpose: Crew launch vehicle
- Stages: 5-segment solid rocket booster first stage, J-2X upper stage
- Payload: Orion CEV to low Earth orbit

## Ares V
- Purpose: Cargo launch vehicle
- Configuration: Shuttle-derived core with RS-68 engines, twin 5-segment SRBs
- Payload: Earth Departure Stage and Altair lunar lander

## Orion Crew Exploration Vehicle
- Named 2006
- Crew capacity: 4-6 astronauts
- Capable of lunar orbit missions lasting up to 21 days
- Service module provides propulsion and life support

## Altair Lunar Lander
- Descent and ascent stages
- Capable of landing 4 crew on the lunar surface
- Designed for up to 7-day surface stays
