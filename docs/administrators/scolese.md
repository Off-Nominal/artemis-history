# Christopher Scolese — Acting NASA Administrator

**Term:** January 20, 2009 – July 17, 2009
**Status:** Acting Administrator

## Background

- NASA Associate Administrator under Griffin
- Career NASA engineer and manager
- Later became director of Goddard Space Flight Center and director of the
  National Reconnaissance Office

## Context

Scolese served during the transition from the Bush to Obama administrations, a
critical period for the future of human spaceflight. The Obama administration
commissioned the **Review of U.S. Human Spaceflight Plans Committee** (Augustine
Committee) during this period, which would ultimately determine Constellation's fate.

The Augustine Committee was announced in May 2009 and delivered its findings in
September 2009, concluding that Constellation was significantly over budget and
behind schedule under its current funding profile.
