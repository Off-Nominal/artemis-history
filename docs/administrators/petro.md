# Janet Petro — Acting NASA Administrator

**Term:** January 20, 2025 – July 9, 2025
**Status:** Acting Administrator

## Background

- Director of Kennedy Space Center since 2021
- First woman to serve as NASA's top leader (acting or permanent)
- Retired U.S. Army Colonel
- MBA from Emory University
- Her appointment surprised some observers as it bypassed Jim Free, NASA's
  Associate Administrator, who would have been the traditional successor

## Key Actions

- Managed ongoing Artemis II launch preparations at Kennedy Space Center
- Oversaw continued program operations during political transition
- Maintained program continuity while the Isaacman nomination stalled in the Senate
- Navigated early DOGE workforce review impacts on NASA centers

## Context

Petro took over during a turbulent political period. The Trump administration had
nominated Jared Isaacman as NASA Administrator in December 2024, but his confirmation
was delayed by Senate holds. Meanwhile, DOGE-driven workforce reviews created
uncertainty across NASA centers. Petro provided stability during this transition.
