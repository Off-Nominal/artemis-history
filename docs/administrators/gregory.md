# Frederick D. Gregory — Acting NASA Administrator

**Term:** February 11, 2005 – April 14, 2005
**Status:** Acting Administrator

## Background

- NASA Deputy Administrator under Sean O'Keefe
- Former astronaut: pilot on STS-51B (1985), commander on STS-33 (1989) and STS-44 (1991)
- First African American to command a Space Shuttle mission
- Stepped in when O'Keefe departed to become chancellor of Louisiana State University

## Context

Gregory served as a caretaker during the transition between O'Keefe and incoming
administrator Michael Griffin. He maintained continuity of the Vision for Space
Exploration and Constellation program planning during this two-month interim period.
