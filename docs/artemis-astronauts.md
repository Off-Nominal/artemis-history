# Artemis Generation Astronaut Team

## Announced: December 9, 2020

At a National Space Council meeting at Kennedy Space Center, Vice President
Pence announces 18 astronauts selected as candidates for early Artemis
missions.

## Selection Criteria

Members were chosen based on performance, qualifications, and mission
requirements. The team includes diverse backgrounds: military pilots,
scientists, engineers, and physicians.

## Assignment

Specific crew assignments for Artemis II and III to be determined.
